## 10. Design Direction (New Theme: "Signal & Momentum")

**Must be different from current Single Grain site:**
- Current: Bright, gradient-heavy, traditional agency look
- New: Dark-mode optional, editorial typography, data-forward, minimal gradients, asymmetric layouts

**Key Visual Elements:**
- Bold section dividers
- "Proof blocks" as prominent cards
- Motion accents (subtle, purposeful)
- Editorial typography hierarchy
- Data/metrics as visual elements
- Productized agency feel (not traditional agency)
